FaceAttend

Our attendance tracking system uses the latest facial recognition technology to accurately and efficiently track attendance at events, meetings, and classes. With our system, there is no need for manual sign-in sheets or card scanners. Simply have attendees look into the camera and our system will automatically record their attendance. The system is easy to use and saves time and resources, making it a valuable tool for any organization looking to streamline their attendance tracking process.
